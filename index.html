Templating

Dust by LinkedIn

WordPress Templates

The markup you know and love.

<?php if ( have_posts() ) : while ( have_posts() ) : the_post(); ?>
  <h2><a href="<?php the_permalink() ?>">
    <?php the_title(); ?></a></h2>
  <div><?php the_content(); ?></div>
<?php endwhile; else: ?>
  <p>No posts to display.</p>
<?php endif; ?>

Dust Templates

Clear syntax leaves PHP in the dust.

{#posts}
  <h2><a href="{link}">{title}</a>
  <div>{content}</div>
{/posts}
{^posts}
  <p>No posts to display.</p>
{/posts}
